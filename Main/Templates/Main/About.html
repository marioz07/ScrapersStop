About Us

ScrapStop is a recycling company in India which provides a comprehensive scrap and waste management services in district, ensuring services from collection and disposal of waste or scrap to recycling and generation of renewable energy. We ensure all discarded scrap or waste are efficiently managed, disposed and recycled



Our Mission

Our mission is to ensure industries, companies, corporates and government agencies to provide a profitable model for complying with both their legal and social responsibilities towards the environment by:

  • Reducing pollution and toxicity
  • Conserving natural resources
  • Reducing product life cycle cost
  • Minimizing carbon footprint

Our aim is to reduce, recycle, reuse of waste or scrap in the most cost effective, various methods or recycling.
